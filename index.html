Questions

How will this work?

Every other year, the International Conference on Appropriate Technology (ICAT) brings together dozens of passionate thinkers and doers in appropriate technology. We're holding this hackathon in cunjunction with the 6th ICAT, which runs from November 25-29 at the Kenyatta University Conference Center. The hackathon will be a 24-hour event for anyone interested in appropriate technology.

This is an opportunity to work with people you wouldn't otherwise; to learn something you wouldn't otherwise; to play with a technology you wouldn't otherwise. Our goal is to create an atmosphere that's conducive to mutual inspiration and productive collaboration.

Do I need to be a programmer to participate?

No! Hackathons are best when people with a variety of knowledge and expertise participate. Hackathons revolve around production, but design, domain expertise, outreach planning, and more all contribute to an effective product.

Will there be food?

We will be providing lunch [from where?] on Saturday. Additionally, we'll have little snacks throughout the night, and a light breakfast in the morning. You are encouraged to eat dinner before the event starts on Friday.

What is Appropriate Technology (AT)?

AT is designed with special consideration for the environmental, ethical, cultural, social and economic aspects of the community it is intended for. With these goals in mind, AT typically requires fewer resources, is easier to maintain, has a lower overall cost and less of an impact on the environment compared to industrialized practices. Most importantly, AT is technology to empower people.

What are Ignite talks?

Ignites are 5-minute talks with 20 slides that auto-advance every 15 seconds. It's a fun, high-energy way to share ideas. Watch Why and How to Give an Ignite Talk to get an idea of the format.

If you're interested in doing a talk, get in touch!

Can I get a ride?

Yes. We'll have a shuttle bus pick up from [iHub?] on Friday at 2:30 PM and drop off at the same place around 4:00 PM on Saturday. If you require transportation, please let us know when you register.
